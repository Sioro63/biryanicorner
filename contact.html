Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an online takeout order at Biryani Corner?

To place an order, visit our Messenger or Instagram page, Browse our menu, tell us your order, and proceed to checkout. Follow the simple steps to provide your contact information and confirm your order.

What payment options are available for online orders?

We accept online payments including credit/debit cards and digital wallets like GCash or Maya. Cash payments are also accepted upon pickup.

Can I customize my order to suit my preferences?

We understand that everyone has unique tastes! When placing your order online, specify spice levels, protein choices, and additional add-ons. Simply specify your preferences before finalizing your order.

What is the estimated time for order preparation and pickup?

Biryani Corner strives to prepare your order with care and efficiency. The estimated time for order preparation and pickup will be provided during the checkout process. You will also receive a confirmation message with the details of your order and pickup time.
